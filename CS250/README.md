## CS 250 Portfolio Reflection

### How do I interpret user needs and implement them into a program? How does creating user stories help with this?

Interpreting user needs starts with understanding what the user is really trying to accomplish, not just what they say on the surface. During this course, I learned that breaking requirements into smaller and clearer goals makes development much easier to manage.

User stories help a lot with this because they focus on the user’s perspective. They explain who the user is, what they need, and why they need it. This makes it easier to understand the purpose behind each feature instead of just building something without context. It also helps reduce confusion within the team and keeps everyone working toward the same goal.

### How do I approach developing programs? What Agile processes do I hope to incorporate into my future development work?

When I approach developing programs, I try to break everything down into smaller pieces instead of thinking about the entire project all at once. This makes the work feel more manageable and helps me stay focused.

Agile practices fit well with this approach. I would like to continue using things like sprint planning, daily stand ups, and retrospectives in the future. These help keep work organized and give regular opportunities to improve. I also think getting feedback often is very important because it allows changes to be made early instead of at the end. Overall, Agile makes development more flexible and easier to adjust when things change.

### What does it mean to be a good team member in software development?

Being a good team member means more than just completing your own tasks. It involves communicating clearly, being dependable, and helping others when needed. During this course, I saw how important it is to stay involved with the team and share updates regularly.

It is also important to be open to feedback and willing to adjust when needed. Not everything works perfectly the first time, so being able to improve as a team is important. Taking responsibility for your work and communicating honestly helps the whole team succeed.
